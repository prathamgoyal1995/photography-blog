The Title

Photography is the process of creating still or moving pictures by recording radiation on a sensitive medium like film or an electronic sensor. ... Light patterns reflected or emitted by objects activate a sensitive chemical or electronic sensor during a timed exposure usually through a photographic lens in a camera.
